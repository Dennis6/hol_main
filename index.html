what we do

We are committed to creating, owning and marketing positive and innovative techniques, images, products and ideas that express our African traditions, culture and heritage.

Locks Extensions

The purpose of doing locs extensions is for anyone of any race, with any length or texture hair who wants to change their look to the natural form of dreadlocks.

Locks

We offer proper loc maintenance to all clients because it is the key to healthy locs. Over twenty years of experience in loc cultivation and maintenance is what makes this bussines stand out from my competition.

Hair Braiding

Cornrows, box braids, and plaits. There are a variety of hair braiding styles. If you're looking for a dazzling dose of natural hair inspiration, you've come to the right place. You'll find many unique styles we specialize in.

Natural Hair Style

Diverse natural styles for all occasions include double-strand twists, comb-coils twist, afro twist out, combination styles such as cornrows with double-strands, braids with flat twists - the options are endless.
